**Portable Hole** adds a simple magical tool that opens temporary passages through solid blocks. Click a surface to clear a short tunnel in that direction, letting you slip through walls, floors, ceilings, and tight spaces without permanently breaking anything. 🌀

After a brief moment, the removed blocks return on their own, making it useful for exploration, quick escapes, hidden entrances, and creative builds where you want movement without lasting damage. ✨

The mod keeps the effect readable with spark particles, a portal-style overlay, and reappearing block feedback, while offering configuration for hole depth, duration, cooldown, visuals, and which blocks can be affected. ⚙️
