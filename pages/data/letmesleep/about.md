**Let Me Sleep** gives you full control over Minecraft's sleeping experience. 🌙 From bedtime restrictions to waking up refreshed, nearly every part of sleeping can be customized to better fit your world and playstyle.

Trouble finding the monster keeping you awake? Beds refusing to work when they should? Want sleeping to heal, clear effects, or simply feel smoother? This mod makes nights more flexible, less frustrating, and far more configurable. ✨
