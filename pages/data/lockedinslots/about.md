**Locked In Slots** helps protect your most important items from accidental inventory mistakes. 🔒 Simply hover a slot and press the lock key to toggle its locked state. Locked slots keep valuable tools, weapons, and other items safely in place until you decide otherwise.

No more accidentally dropping your pickaxe, moving your sword into a chest, or replacing an important hotbar slot during a hectic adventure. Once a slot is locked, its contents stay exactly where they belong. 🛡️

Best of all, the mod is fully client side and works in both singleplayer and on multiplayer servers. ✨
