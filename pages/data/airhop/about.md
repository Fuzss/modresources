**Air Hop** adds a simple but satisfying new movement enchantment that lets you jump again while already in the air. 🪽 It gives players more control during exploration, parkour, combat, and tricky landings.

The mod is built around the new enchantment, which can be applied to leg armour and grants extra mid-air jumps based on enchantment level. Each hop feels responsive, with optional cloud particles and a playful sound effect for extra feedback. ✨

Turn a single jump into a chain of mid-air bursts, correcting mistakes, clearing gaps, and extending movement in ways vanilla traversal never allows. ⚡
