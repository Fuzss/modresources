* 🎮 **Console-Inspired Layout:** Recreates the elevated hotbar style found in Bedrock Edition and Legacy Console Edition.
* ✨ **Floating Hotbar Appearance:** Moves the hotbar away from the bottom screen edge, making it appear to hover in place.
* 🖼️ **Fully Visible Selection Highlight:** Allows the complete hotbar selection sprite to render instead of being partially cut off by the screen border.
* 🎛️ **Adjustable Positioning:** Freely configure the hotbar offset to create your preferred HUD layout.
* 📈 **Improved Experience Display:** Optionally moves the experience level indicator above the experience bar, matching the console-style interface.
* 🔧 **Mod Compatibility:** Supports shifting additional modded hotbar overlays together with the hotbar.
