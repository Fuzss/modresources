+ 🪨 **Expanded Recipe Menu:** The stonecutter now displays up to 21 recipes at once instead of vanilla's 12.
+ 🧱 **Improved Layout:** Inputs, outputs, and recipe selection are rearranged for a cleaner workflow.
+ ✨ **Smoother Scrolling:** Recipe lists scroll more smoothly for faster and more comfortable browsing.
