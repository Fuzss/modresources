**Stonecutting Upgrade** gives the stonecutter menu the overhaul it deserves. 🪨 With a larger recipe selection area, smoother scrolling, and a more convenient layout, stonecutting finally feels comfortable to use again.

No more constantly scrolling through cramped recipe lists or reselecting the same block over and over. This mod remembers your last recipe and includes quick refill controls to keep crafting flowing smoothly. ✨

Best of all, the mod is fully client side, making it easy to use anywhere without requiring server installation. ⚙️
