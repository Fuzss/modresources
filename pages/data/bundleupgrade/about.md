**Bundle Upgrade** enhances Minecraft's vanilla bundles with a variety of quality of life improvements that make managing stored items faster, easier, and more intuitive. 📦

Vanilla bundles already support inventory interactions, but accessing large bundles quickly can become cumbersome. This mod removes the limit on visible contents, allowing every stored item to be viewed directly in the bundle tooltip. Browse through items using the mouse wheel or keyboard arrow keys, then extract exactly what you need without rearranging the rest. 🎯

The mod builds upon Minecraft's existing bundle interface rather than replacing it, preserving the familiar vanilla experience while adding more powerful navigation, improved visibility, and additional inventory management features. ✨

Unlike simple tooltip enhancements, this is not a client only mod. It adds real inventory interactions and must be installed on both the client and server. If you only want improved bundle previews, check out [Bundles Beyond](https://modrinth.com/mod/bundles-beyond) by [7410](https://modrinth.com/user/7410). 💡
