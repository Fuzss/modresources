**Hopper Gadgetry** expands Minecraft’s item transport with practical hopper-style blocks for cleaner, smarter automation. It adds chutes, ducts, and grated hoppers that help move items exactly where you need them without overcomplicating your builds ⚙️.

The mod is especially useful for sorting systems, compact farms, and rail-based collection setups. Chutes quickly pass dropped items into containers, ducts move items through directed connections, and grated hoppers add simple item filtering for more controlled collection 🔎.
