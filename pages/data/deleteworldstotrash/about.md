**Delete Worlds To Trash** changes how world deletion works in Minecraft. 📁 Instead of permanently deleting a world, it moves it to your system trash or recycle bin, giving you a chance to recover it if you change your mind.

No more accidental losses or permanent mistakes. 🧠 If a world was important after all, you can easily bring it back later from the trash.

Simple, safe, and built to save you from regret. ✨
