**Open Together** makes matching doors, trapdoors, and fence gates open as a group when placed next to each other. Open one block, and connected neighbours follow automatically, making double doors and wider entrances feel natural to use. 🚪

The mod removes repetitive clicks during everyday gameplay while preserving vanilla behaviour and building styles. It works with manual interactions and can also support vanilla mechanics such as redstone when installed on the server. ⚡

Unlike most similar solutions, the mod can be installed on the client, the server, or both. Server installation provides the most complete and consistent experience for all players. Client-only use also works on multiplayer servers without the mod, but only for your own manual interactions and not for server-side effects like redstone. 🌐
