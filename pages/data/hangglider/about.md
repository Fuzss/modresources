**Hang Glider** adds a hang glider to soar through the skies. 🪂 Simply deploy it, leap from a cliff, and glide through your world with smooth and natural flight controls. Scenic travel has never felt this good. ☁️

Wind and movement create a more immersive flying experience, encouraging you to stay on course as you glide through the air. Want a little more speed? Reinforce your glider for faster and sturdier flights. ⚙️

With polished models, immersive visuals, and highly configurable mechanics, Hang Glider offers a fun and stylish way to take to the skies. ✨
