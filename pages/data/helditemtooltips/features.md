* 🏷️ **Enhanced Held Item Tooltips:** Displays detailed information directly above your hotbar when switching items.
* ✨ **Rich Item Details:** View enchantments, durability, potion effects, attributes, container contents, and more at a glance.
* 📦 **Container Previews:** Quickly inspect the contents of shulker boxes and other supported storage items.
* 🧪 **Useful Item Information:** Shows details for potions, fireworks, maps, banners, books, paintings, and many other special items.
* 📏 **Smart Line Priorities:** Important information is shown first when space is limited.
* 🎨 **Custom Appearance:** Configure colors, text formatting, background style, size, position, and display duration.
* 🔧 **Per-Component Control:** Enable, disable, reorder, and customize individual tooltip sections independently.
* 🧩 **Mod Friendly:** Supports additional tooltip information provided by other mods.
