**Held Item Tooltips** enhances the tooltip shown above your hotbar when switching items, giving you far more information at a glance. ✨

See enchantments, durability, potion effects, shulker box contents, firework details, map IDs, item attributes, and much more without opening additional screens. Whether you're organizing storage, checking equipment, or browsing your inventory, the information you need is always right in front of you. 🔍

Everything is fully customizable. Adjust the appearance, choose which information should be displayed, control the order of tooltip sections, and tailor the experience to fit your needs. ⚙️
