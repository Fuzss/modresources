**Easy Anvils** makes anvils more convenient, less frustrating, and far more practical to use. 🔨 Items stay inside the interface when you close it, while the contents are rendered directly on the block for a more immersive experience.

The mod also reworks several vanilla anvil mechanics that can make repairing and upgrading gear unnecessarily tedious. Prior work penalties become much less restrictive, repair costs stay reasonable for longer, and several common actions are made more affordable. ✨

From repairing damaged anvils to free renaming and formatting support, this mod delivers a collection of quality-of-life improvements that make anvils feel better throughout the entire game.
