**Completionists Index** adds a handy client side checklist for every item you have collected in your world. 📚 It offers a fresh and convenient way to track your progress toward collecting everything the game has to offer.

Items are sorted by mod and grouped by creative mode tab, with progress bars for each category showing how much you have already discovered. 🔍 A built in search function helps you quickly find exactly what you are looking for.

Access the menu anytime through a dedicated button in your inventory or the pause menu. ✨ Tracked items are fully customizable, making Completionists Index perfect for collectors, completionists, and proud hoarders alike. 🏆
