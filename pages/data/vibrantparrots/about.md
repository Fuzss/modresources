**Vibrant Parrots** expands Minecraft's parrots with new colors, breeding, eggs, and bird cages, giving these jungle companions a much richer pet experience. 🦜

Discover 11 additional parrot variants for a total of 16 colorful birds to find, collect, and breed. Parrots can now lay eggs that hatch into chicks, making it possible to build and grow your own flock. 🐣

The mod also makes parrots easier to travel with. Carry tamed birds in portable cages, and customize shoulder sitting behavior to take your cute companions wherever your adventures lead. 🎒

Parrot variants finally become fully data-driven and can be extended using data packs with custom textures, allowing entirely new colors and species to be added. 🎨
