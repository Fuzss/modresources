* 🎨 **Dye-Based Channels:** Create unique linked storage networks using three dye colours. Configure channels directly on the chest or craft dyes into linked chests and pouches.
* 📦 **Shared Remote Storage:** Any linked chest with the same channel opens the same inventory, no matter where it is placed.
* 🔒 **Personal Channels:** Convert a channel into player-specific storage for private access with double the inventory space.
* 🎒 **Linked Pouch:** Carry portable access to a selected linked chest channel from anywhere.
* 🧭 **Easy Channel Copying:** Sneak-use a linked pouch on a linked chest to bind it to that chest's channel.
* ⚙️ **Hopper Compatible:** Fully supports hoppers and other automation for easy item transport and storage systems.
* ✨ **Visual Channel Feedback:** Chests and pouches display their selected dye colours, making channels easy to identify at a glance.
