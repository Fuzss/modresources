**Mindful Darkness** adds a fully automatic dark mode to your game. 🌙 Darken menus and interfaces instantly without requiring any texture changes or resource packs.

Toggle it on or off directly in game and customize the look to match your preferences. 🎨 Choose from different darkening algorithms, adjust the darkness intensity, and fine tune the effect with ease.

A simple texture control system is also included, letting you decide exactly which textures are affected. ⚙️ Comfortable, flexible, and easy on the eyes. ✨
