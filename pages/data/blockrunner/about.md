**Block Runner** makes movement more exciting by giving certain blocks their own speed boosts whenever players or mobs walk on them. 🚀

Paths finally do more than just look nice. Dirt paths and stone bricks can make you move faster, making roads and walkways feel more useful and rewarding. 🛤️

Want full control? You can customize speed values for any block using the `blockrunner:block_speeds` block [data map](https://docs.neoforged.net/docs/resources/server/datamaps/). ⚙️

No new items, no complicated mechanics, just faster movement where it makes sense. 💨 Feels right at home in Minecraft! 🎮
