**Skeleton AI Fix** removes the frustrating and buggy strafing behavior of Skeletons. 🏹 No more walking backwards, falling off cliffs, or getting stuck in tight corners. Instead, they stand their ground and focus on landing deadly shots, firing faster the closer you get. Their aim sharpens as you approach, creating a real challenge without awkward movement. 🎯

All bow using Skeletons, from the classic Skeleton to the Stray, stay focused and deliver smoother, more intense battles. ⚔️ Get ready for faster arrows and enemies that are not distracted by the terrain. Whether you rush in or keep your distance, every fight becomes a tense and thrilling shootout. 💀
