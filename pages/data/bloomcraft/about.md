**Bloomcraft** introduces charming flower covered cows and chickens that thrive in flower forests. 🌸 Each one carries blooms on its back, reflecting the beauty and variety of its home biome. As they wander, they occasionally leave behind matching flowers, spreading color wherever they go. 🌼

Just like their mushroom cousins, they can be milked for suspicious stew. 🥣 To make the world feel even livelier, several flowers from Minecraft Earth have also returned, bringing new beauty to fields and meadows. 🌷

Best of all, flower themed mob variants are fully data driven. ⚙️ You can easily add your own through a simple data pack, making customization and mod compatibility easy peasy. ✨
