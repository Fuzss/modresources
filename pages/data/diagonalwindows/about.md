**Diagonal Windows** lets glass panes and iron bars connect diagonally, opening up new ways to shape windows and detailed builds. 🪟 Small change, but it makes structures feel noticeably smoother and more natural.

It also supports blocks from other mods whenever possible, with full control over exclusions through the `diagonalwindows:non_diagonal_windows` block tag. ⚙️ Works cleanly with most resource packs without extra setup.

Part of the Diagonal Blocks family, alongside [Diagonal Fences](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/diagonal-fences) and [Diagonal Walls](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/diagonal-walls), expanding diagonal building in different directions. ✨
