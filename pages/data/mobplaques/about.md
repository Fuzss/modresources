**Mob Plaques** adds compact status displays above mobs, letting you quickly see important information without cluttering your screen. Health, armor, armor toughness, and air supply are shown using familiar Minecraft-style icons that feel right at home in the game. ❤️

Whether you're fighting monsters, managing animals, exploring underwater, or taking on bosses, the mod helps you judge a mob's condition at a glance. The plaques stay small and unobtrusive while still providing the information that matters most. 🛡️

This mod is designed to fit a wide range of playstyles, with flexible visibility options, support for many different entity types, and an in-game toggle whenever you want a cleaner view. 👁️
