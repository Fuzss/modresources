**Bag Of Holding** adds magical portable storage that feels right at home in Minecraft. Carry your belongings in stylish bags, access them wherever you go, and spend less time fighting a cluttered inventory. 🎒

Choose between three bag tiers offering increasing storage capacity, from a compact leather pouch to an enormous golden bag capable of carrying entire building projects. Open bags directly from your hotbar or interact with them seamlessly from within your inventory. ✨

Inspired by modern bundle mechanics, the mod lets you browse contents, insert items, extract exactly what you need, and manage storage without constantly opening separate menus. Powerful tooltip previews and intuitive controls make every bag feel fast and convenient to use. 🎯

For adventurers who value their belongings, bags can even be enchanted with *Preservation*, allowing them to survive death at the cost of enchantment levels. 💎
