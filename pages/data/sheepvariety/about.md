**Sheep Variety** is here to fix a great injustice! 🐑 Chickens, pigs, and cows got fancy biome variants in vanilla, but sheep were left behind. No more! Sheep finally gain brand new models and textures based on the biome they live in. Woolly sheep for colder regions, horned sheep for warmer climates, and flocks that actually fit their homes. 🌍

No gameplay changes, just better looking sheep wherever you go. ✨ From chilly mountains to warm badlands, every climate gets a little more personality. Explore, discover, and meet sheep that finally look like they belong. Stylish, fluffy, and long overdue. 🌿
